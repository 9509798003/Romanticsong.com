Jubin Nutriyal

Jubin Nautiyal is an Indian playback singer and performer. He was awarded Upcoming Male Vocalist of the Year at 8th Mirchi Music Awards for his song "Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata" from Bajrangi Bhaijaan. He also won the Rising Musical Star Award at Zee Business Awards.
